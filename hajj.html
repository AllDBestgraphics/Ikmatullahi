HAJJ REQUIRMENTS

To embark on the sacred journey of Hajj, below is a list of things you must have prepared before you set out to perform your Hajj. Many of these are conditions set out by Saudi Arabian authorities. CREDENTIALS

  1. •International passport
  2. •Passports must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of travel
  3. •Health certificate
  4. •Vaccination certificates (Covid-19, Yellow fever, etc
  5. •8 recent passport-sized color photographs
  6. •Payment of deposit/balance by the due date (prior to travel)
  7. • Hajj Visa

Kindly note that we can assist you in obtaining these documents and guide you through the necessary steps to ensure a smooth and hassle-free process.

Please remember that you are also bound by general traveling, airport and airline rules/laws not listed above.
